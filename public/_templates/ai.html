AI Use Statement

I do use AI tools to generate some of the content published on this website. Below are places and situations where AI is used, and those where it is not.

AI is used

  • Hero images for posts
  • Internal images on some posts
  • Audio/song version of the website license

AI is not used

  • Generating or writing code for the website
  • Writing content for the website
  • Designs for the website

Is your art stolen?

If you feel like your art have been stolen or scraped by AI, first I'm sorry.

Although I cannot remove your art from the datasets used to train these AI models, I can remove the results from my website.

If you would like me to do so, please contact me at me@madcampos.dev.

If you have the technical knowledge, feel free to open an issue on GitHub.
